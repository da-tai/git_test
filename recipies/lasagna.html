Lasagna

This caldo de pollo turned out delicious on my first attempt! I like to serve this chicken vegetable soup with warm corn tortillas and a side of Spanish rice and refried beans.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds chicken leg quarters
  • 2 gallons water
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons salt

Steps

  1. Step 1: Place chicken legs into a large stockpot; pour water over chicken. Add minced garlic, salt, and garlic powder.
  2. Step 2: Cover and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce to a simmer and cook until chicken meat falls off the bones, 1 to 2 hours.
